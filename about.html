COMPANY

Chargily Is A Fintech Company, Specializing In The Field Of Facilitating Payment Methods And Ecommerce. Offer Users Under Its Electronic Platforms Multitudes Services Such As Mobile Payment, Credit Top-Ups, Prepaid Cards, Bill Payments… Etc. For Companies And Professionals, Chargily Provides Online Payment Gateways On Their Websites And Mobile Applications Such As Open Source Packages, Plugins And Apis. In Addition To The Installation Of Interbank Electronic Payment Devices Such As EPT, POS, Mpos...Etc.
